## Background

Triple Threat was Donner's first major artist-label collaboration — a three-channel multi-effects pedal designed in partnership with Jack White's Third Man Records. The campaign anchored Donner's NAMM 2024 launch and signaled the brand's move from value-tier hardware into named partnerships with culturally significant musicians.

## What I led

- Pre-NAMM media outreach and review unit allocation across Tier 1 outlets (Guitar World, Premier Guitar, Guitar Player, MusicRadar)
- On-site press coordination at NAMM 2024, including video and photo content with Third Man's team
- Post-show review sequencing and follow-through across print, web, and YouTube

## Outcomes

- 9+ Tier 1 reviews and feature stories within the first 60 days
- Coverage in Premier Guitar, Guitar World, Guitar.com, Music Inc, Worship Musician, Cutaway Guitar Magazine, Vintage Guitar, MMR, Music Connection
