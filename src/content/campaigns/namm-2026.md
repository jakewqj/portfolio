## Background

NAMM 2026 was the biggest single-show release window of the year — five products, three product lines, two cultural collaborations (MIYAVI signature, Third Man follow-on). PR scope: pre-show embargoes, on-floor press visits, post-show review pipeline.

## What I led

- Pre-show embargo coordination (12+ outlets under embargo until press day)
- On-site media tours and demo coordination across the booth
- Translated press kit (EN / ZH / JP) for the MIYAVI launch
- Post-NAMM review sequencing — staggered releases over 6 weeks to maintain momentum

## Outcomes

- Coverage across Guitar World, MusicRadar, Synth Anatomy, MIDIFAN, Matrixsynth, and 18 other outlets
- MIYAVI Double Swords secured Japanese-market launch coverage on Drum & Rhythm
- L1 synth picked up by the synth vertical press (Synthtopia, Sound on Sound)
