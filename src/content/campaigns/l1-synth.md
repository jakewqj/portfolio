## Background

L1 was Donner's first product targeted at the modular / hardware synth community — a market with its own dedicated press, dense subreddits, and gear-forum ecosystem. PR strategy: build credibility with the synth verticals first (Synth Anatomy, Synthtopia, Matrixsynth, Sound on Sound), then expand into mainstream music production media (MusicRadar, MusicTech).

## What I led

- New media-relationship building across the synth vertical (no prior contact at most outlets at launch)
- Beijing Modular Festival booth coordination (2024, 2025, 2026)
- NAMM 2025 + 2026 L1 demo coordination on the show floor
- Sustained content cadence: launch, firmware updates, signature patch packs

## Outcomes

- 38 unique placements across 3 years
- Coverage in Synth Anatomy, Synthtopia, Matrixsynth, Sound on Sound, MusicRadar, MIDIFAN
- Established Donner as a recognized name in modular / semi-modular synthesis at consumer price points
