## Background

The HUSH series became Donner's most consistent guitar story between 2024 and 2026. Each iteration (HUSH-X, HUSH-I, HUSH-X PRO, HUSH-I PRO, HUSH-I EVO 2) was treated as a discrete press cycle, with shared messaging anchors: silent practice, lightweight travel, modern player ergonomics.

## What I led

- Per-launch pitch angles tailored to silent-guitar incumbents (Yamaha SLG-200, Traveler Guitar)
- Sustained editorial relationships across Guitar World, MusicRadar, Premier Guitar, and Guitar Interactive
- Round-up placements in "best silent guitar" / "best travel guitar" buyer's guides — long-tail SEO play
- Bilingual press kits for Chinese launches via MIDIFAN and 叉烧网

## Outcomes

- 30+ unique placements across 4 years
- Round-up entries in MusicRadar, TomsGuide, Performer Magazine
- Repeat reviews in Guitar World and Premier Guitar across multiple SKUs
